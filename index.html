About Spider-Man

Spider-Man

Spider-Man is my favorite fictional superhero. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko for Marvel Comics, Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, gained superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider.

More About Spider-Man

He lives in New York City and works as a freelance photographer for the Daily Bugle newspaper. By day, he leads a normal life as Peter Parker, but by night, he fights crime as Spider-Man, using his agility, strength, and web-shooters to combat villains and protect the innocent.

Spider-Man has faced numerous adversaries, including the Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and Venom, but he always strives to uphold the values of responsibility and justice instilled in him by his late Uncle Ben.
